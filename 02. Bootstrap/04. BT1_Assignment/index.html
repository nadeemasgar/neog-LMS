Welcome to the World of BTS

BTS, also known as Bangtan Sonyeondan or the Bangtan Boys, is a South Korean boy band that has revolutionized the music industry and captured the hearts of millions worldwide. Since their debut on June 13, 2013, under Big Hit Entertainment (now HYBE Corporation), the seven-member group has redefined success in the global music scene through their talent, authenticity, and impactful messages.

BTS is more than just a musical group; they are a cultural and social phenomenon that has redefined the boundaries of global artistry. Through their dedication, innovation, and genuine connection with their fans, BTS continues to inspire millions, proving that music truly knows no limits.
