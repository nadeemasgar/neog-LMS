Biography

Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi, commonly known as Mahatma Gandhi,was a prominent leader in the Indain independence movement. He was born on October 2, 1869, in Porbandar,India. Gandhi is best for his philosophy of nonviolent resistance and civil disobedience, which he called "Satyagraha.

Philosophy

Ahimsa(NonViolence)

Mahatma Gandhi believed in the power of nonviolence as a mean to bring about social and political change. He Practiced and preached the principle of "Ahimsa" which advocate nonviolence resistance even in the face of oppression

Satyagraha (Truth Force)

Gandhi's Concept of "Satyagraha"emphasized the pursuits of truth and justice through nonviolence means. It became a cornerstone of his strugge for India's independence.

Legacy

Gandhi's legacy includes a wide range of ideas ranging from his dream of ideal India (or Rama Rajya), economics, environmentalism, women's rights, animal rights, spirituality, the truth, nonviolence, asceticism and others.
