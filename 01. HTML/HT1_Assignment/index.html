Cricket Records

Cricket boasts remarkable records across formats. Sachin Tendulkar holds the most international runs (34,357) and centuries (100). In Tests, Brian Lara scored the highest individual score (400*), while Muttiah Muralitharan claimed the most wickets (800).

International Honors

Legends like Sachin Tendulkar, Don Bradman, and Muttiah Muralitharan have been inducted into the ICC Hall of Fame, celebrating their lifelong contributions.

Records

Cricket's history is filled with iconic records. Sachin Tendulkar leads with 100 international centuries and 34,357 runs. Muttiah Muralitharan holds the record for most wickets, with 800 in Tests and 1,347 overall. Rohit Sharma scored the highest ODI individual score of 264 and three double centuries. Brian Lara achieved the highest Test score with 400*. In T20Is, Aaron Finch has the top score of 172, while Virat Kohli leads in runs and Shakib Al Hasan in wickets. AB de Villiers smashed the fastest ODI century in 31 balls, and Chris Gayle dominates six-hitting records across formats.
