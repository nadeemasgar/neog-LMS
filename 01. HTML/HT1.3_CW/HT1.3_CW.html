Festivals

Diwali

Diwali, the Festival of Lights is India's most celebrated festivals.

Holi

Holi, the Festival of Colors, is known for its vibrant celebration

Eid

Eid is a significant Muslim festival marked by prayers and feasting.

Places to Visit

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an iconic symbol of love and a UNESCO world heritage site.

Jaipur

Jaipur, often referred to as the Pink City, is known for its historic sites, including:

  • Amer Fort
  • Hawa Mahal
  • City Palace

Kerala

Kerala is famous for its serene backwaters and lush green ladscapes.

Indian Cuisine

Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a creamy and flavourful indian dish.

Biryani

Biryani is a fragment and aromatic rice dish with various regional variations. Notable ingredients include:

  1. Basmati rice
  2. Chicken or lamb (vegetables for a vegetarian version)
  3. Spices (cumin, coriander and garam masala)
  4. Yogurt ans saffron for flavour and color

Dosa

Dosa is a South indian crispy pancake made with fermented rice and lentil batter.
